About Mutungo Islamic School

Providing Quality Education with Strong Islamic Values

Our History

Mutungo Islamic School is a day and boarding mixed school, nursery and primary having both secular and theology studies. We are located in Mutungo subcounty Nakawa Division in Kampala District, operating under the Ministry of Education and Sports. Mutungo Islamic School admits pupils from nursery to primary both boys and girls

Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To be a leading school in providing morallu upright and dedicated citizen

We educate our pupils not only basing on the National curriculum but also giving opportunities to each child to express and involve him/herself in the learning environment using his/her mind., soul and body expressions. The school gives and serves the best education to pupils in the arena of Religious affiliation for the wellness of the society and for their future.

Our Mission: To provide quality Education and enhance skills for self reliance.

Using the Uganda National Curriculum and syllabus designed for implementation in schools, we additionally provide better teaching teachnics that enable the pupils not only to be confident but also to be able to broaden up his brain in this competitive world hence a self-reliant citizen of the country.

Our Islamic Values

We instill key Islamic principles such as honesty, respect, discipline, and community service in our students. Through daily prayers, Quran studies, and ethical teachings, we guide students to lead lives rooted in faith and morality.

Our Commitment to Safety

- We prohibit chidren from walking alone, being accompained by strangers or getting free lifts/gifts from unknown people.

- We prohibit chidren from coming to school with phones, jewelries sych as rings, chains, necklaces and electronic gadgets.

- Maintain cleanliness and smartness at all times.

- Pupils in boarding are not allowed to be visited by unknown people, friends or relatives ubless alerted and given authority by the parent/guardian through writing and the school must be aware of that.
