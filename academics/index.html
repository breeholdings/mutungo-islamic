Academics at Mutungo Islamic School

Fostering Excellence in Education & Islamic Learning

Our Curriculum

We offer a well-rounded curriculum that integrates national educational standards with strong Islamic teachings. Our programs include:

  • Nursery Section: Early Childhood Development, Literacy, Numeracy, Religious Education, and Creative Activities.
  • Primary School: Mathematics, English, Science, Social Studies, Islamic Studies, and more.
  • Islamic Studies: Quran memorization, Fiqh, Hadith, and Arabic language studies.

Our Educational Approach

We believe in a balanced approach that blends academic excellence with hands-on learning. Our key methods include:

  • Interactive and student-centered learning
  • Practical application of knowledge through projects
  • Integration of Islamic ethics and moral teachings in all subjects
  • Encouraging critical thinking and creativity

Special Programs

We offer unique programs that set our school apart:

  • Quranic Studies: Students engage in daily recitations and memorization.
  • Hifz Program: A dedicated course for students who wish to memorize the entire Quran.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Science clubs, debating teams, and Islamic discussions.

Examinations & Results

We conduct regular assessments to ensure academic progress:

  • Termly exams and progress tests
  • Continuous assessment for holistic learning
  • Parent-Teacher meetings to review performance
  • Outstanding students are recognized and awarded

Student Support

We provide various support systems to help our students succeed:

  • Guidance and counseling services
  • One-on-one tutoring for struggling students
  • Mentorship programs led by teachers and senior students
