Admissions at Mutungo Islamic School

Join our institution and embark on a journey of excellence and Islamic values.

Admission Requirements

To enroll at Mutungo Islamic School, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Nursery Section: Age 3 and above, birth certificate.
  • Primary School: Age 6 and above, birth certificate, previous school report.
  • Completed application form with passport-sized photos.

For more details about the requirements, download the full requirements structure here.

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission:

  1. Download the application form here.
  2. Fill out the form with accurate information.
  3. Attach required documents (birth certificate, school reports, etc.).
  4. Attach a signed Rules and Regulations document. You can download it here.
  5. Attach a signed Code Of Conduct document. You can download it here.
  6. Submit the form to the school office or email it to admissions@mutungoislamicps.com.
  7. Attend the scheduled interview or assessment.
  8. Receive admission confirmation and proceed with registration.
