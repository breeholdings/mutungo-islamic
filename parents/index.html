For Parents

Your Child's Safety, Health, and Development Are Our Priority

Welcome Parents!

At Mutungo Islamic School, we value our collaboration with parents in nurturing our students' academic and personal growth. Below are important guidelines and information to ensure a safe and healthy environment for all students.

Expectations from Parents

  • Drop-off/Pick-up Times: Ensure your child arrives between 7:00 AM and 8:00 AM, and is picked up by 3:00 PM.
  • Approved Visitors: Only authorized visitors may enter the school premises. Please notify the administration in advance.
  • Active Communication: Stay in touch with teachers and school management through parent-teacher meetings or school communications.

Safety Guidelines

  • No Sharp Objects: Children are not permitted to bring sharp items (e.g., scissors, knives) to school.
  • Proper Labeling: All personal items, including clothes and stationery, must be clearly labeled with your child’s name.
  • Supervision: Ensure that children are accompanied by a responsible adult during drop-off and pick-up times.

Health & Hygiene Protocols

  • Regular Handwashing: Ensure your child practices regular handwashing, especially before eating and after using the toilet.
  • Illness Reporting: Notify the school if your child is unwell and unable to attend class.
  • Healthy Snacks: Encourage healthy snacks such as fruits and vegetables in the child’s lunchbox.

Emergency Contacts & Procedures

  • Emergency Contacts: Please provide the school with up-to-date emergency contact information for you and other guardians.
  • Evacuation Plan: In case of an emergency, the school has a clear evacuation plan. Parents will be informed promptly.
  • Accident Reporting: In the event of an accident, parents will be immediately contacted for further action.
